About Estate & Co

Fifteen years of matching
people with the right walls.

We're a small team that believes buying a home should feel like a decision, not a gamble.

Our Beginning

Started at a kitchen table in 2010.

Three friends, one rented office in Karachi, and a shared frustration with how opaque the local property market was. We started Estate & Co to do the boring things properly: verify titles, photograph honestly, and never list a property we wouldn't buy ourselves.

Sixteen years later we've grown to three cities and thirty-two agents, but the standard hasn't moved.
